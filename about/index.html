About Us

Roscoe Labs is a founder-led Bay Area company dedicated to the ideal of independent publishing for all.

Our flagship product is the Beams framework for serving web content securely via the HTTPS protocol. It's free to use under MIT license, and we welcome contributions of all kinds from the community to its continued development.

We monetize Beams through development, consulting, and project management work for clients who want to enhance their digital presence. If you think our experienced team can help, please drop us an email today. We're glad to talk more about your needs.
