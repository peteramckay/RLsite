A new chapter for Roscoe Labs

Effective Aug. 15, 2016, co-founders Peter McKay and Lawrence Patrick will step down from their day-to-day roles at Roscoe Labs as chief product officer and CEO, respectively, to pursue other opportunities. Co-founder Katrina Miles will become the new CEO.

When we started Roscoe Labs more than six years ago, we set out to crowdsource local news by leveraging mobile devices. Then we pivoted to our current mission to equip journalists and publishers with secure, customizable, and open tools to protect free speech and increase independence.

We have learned a great deal from this process, and intend to apply what we have learned to make Roscoe Labs a better company in the months and years ahead.

As we go forward independently, we thank you all again for your support over the years. It has meant a lot to all three of us.
