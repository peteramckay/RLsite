Help us make digital media better.

The Roscoe team accepts -- and greatly appreciates -- bitcoin donations to support our development of open source tools for creators and consumers of great online content. Our open projects, like the Beams framework for making content-focused websites, are free for anyone to use and improve upon.

Donations go toward things like server fees, legal fees, and developer expenses.

Please note, donations aren't tax-deductible in the U.S. That said, we guarantee that making the web just a little better for everyone is a reward unto itself.
